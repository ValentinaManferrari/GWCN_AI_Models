Climate Change Evolution

Some say climate change is the biggest threat of our age while others say it’s a myth based on dodgy science. The scope of this research is to visualize average temperatures by country and process them with the help of AI models to make predictions for the future so you can form your own view.

The Dataset

All data used for this exercise has been taken from Kaggle database "Climate Change: Earth Surface Temperature Data - Exploring global temperatures since 1750" [1]

Let's read the data from the "GlobalTemperatures.csv" file, which has a monthly Earth’s temperature and plot it on the chart. This line graph includes confidence intervals to shows how measurements of temperature have become more accurate in the last decades. Since the 1980 it is obvious that there has been a continuous increase in the overall mean annual temperature.

Feel free to click on the graph to open it in Plotly Visual Studio and have more insights on the represented data.

A look into the Future

Now we want to use the above shown data to predic how the average land temperature will change in the next 50 years. For this task Time Series Forcasting has been used, specifically an ARIMA model with parameters (0, 1, 1). the resulting RMSE is 0.183

Selet a country in the drop down menu to see what are the forecasted average temperatures there for the next 50 years.
